# Accepted Risks

This document records risks that have been formally accepted.

## Acceptance Criteria

A risk may be accepted if:

- residual risk is low or medium after mitigations
- a documented justification is provided
- a risk owner is assigned
- a review date is set

## Currently Accepted Risks

None at this time. All risks are in open or mitigated status.

## Review Schedule

Accepted risks are reviewed every 6 months.
