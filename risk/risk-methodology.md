# Risk Methodology

## Approach

This risk register follows a qualitative risk assessment approach.

## Likelihood Scale

| Value | Definition |
|---|---|
| low | Unlikely to occur given current controls |
| medium | Could occur; some conditions are present |
| high | Likely to occur; conditions are favorable |

## Impact Scale

| Value | Definition |
|---|---|
| low | Minor disruption, no data loss |
| medium | Moderate disruption or data exposure |
| high | Significant disruption or data breach |
| critical | Severe impact on safety, compliance or operations |

## Inherent Risk Matrix

| | Low Impact | Medium Impact | High Impact | Critical Impact |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Low Likelihood | low | low | medium | high |
| Medium Likelihood | low | medium | high | critical |
| High Likelihood | medium | high | high | critical |

## Risk Treatment

- Mitigate: apply controls to reduce likelihood or impact.
- Accept: document justification and owner.
- Transfer: insurance or third-party.
- Avoid: eliminate the activity.
