# Privacy Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes privacy principles and controls for all data processing activities.

## Principles

- Data minimization: collect only what is strictly necessary.
- Purpose limitation: use data only for the documented purpose.
- Storage limitation: retain data only as long as required.
- Accuracy: maintain data accuracy and correct errors.
- Security: apply appropriate technical and organizational measures.

## GDPR Alignment

Processing activities must comply with GDPR Article 5 principles and Article 25 (privacy by design).

## Data Inventory

All data assets must be registered in privacy/data-inventory.yaml.
