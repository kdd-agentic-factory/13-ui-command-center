# Incident Response Policy

## Purpose

This policy defines the process for detecting, responding to and recovering from security incidents.

## Severity Levels

| Severity | Response Time |
|---|---|
| SEV-1 Critical | Immediate |
| SEV-2 High | 4 hours |
| SEV-3 Medium | 2 business days |

## Response Phases

1. Detect
2. Triage
3. Contain
4. Eradicate
5. Recover
6. Postmortem

## Postmortem Requirements

Every SEV-1 and SEV-2 incident requires a postmortem with timeline, root cause, corrective actions, and archived evidence.
