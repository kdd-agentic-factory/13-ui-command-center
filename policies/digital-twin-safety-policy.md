# Digital Twin Safety Policy

## Core Rule

Simulation results are never a substitute for real-world validation. The digital twin is a tool for exploration and hypothesis testing, not for approving race decisions.

## Allowed Use

The Digital Twin may: simulate setup scenarios, run what-if analyses, generate simulation reports, provide simulation evidence for crew chief review.

## Forbidden Use

The Digital Twin must not: apply real setup changes, be used as the sole basis for a critical race decision, generate results presented as real-world validated.

## Output Labeling

All simulation outputs must be labeled as simulation results with scenario parameters, confidence and uncertainty bounds, and explicit disclaimer that human review is required.
