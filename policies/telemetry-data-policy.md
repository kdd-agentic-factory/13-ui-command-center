# Telemetry Data Policy

## Purpose

This policy governs the collection, use, retention and protection of telemetry data.

## Classification

All telemetry session data is classified as restricted.

## Allowed Use

Telemetry data may be used for: race engineering analysis, digital twin simulation, KDD data pipeline processing, paper evidence generation.

## Prohibited Use

Telemetry data must not be shared externally without approval, used to identify individuals without consent, or stored in public repositories.

## Retention

- Telemetry session data: 12 months
- Aggregated processed data: 36 months

## Export

Export requires data owner approval, data classification verification, minimization review and documented export purpose.
