# Evidence Retention Policy

## Purpose

This policy ensures that evidence required for reproducibility, compliance and incident response is retained for the required period.

## Evidence Categories

| Category | Retention |
|---|---|
| Approval records | 36 months |
| Security scan reports | 36 months |
| Incident reports and postmortems | 36 months |
| Paper experiment artifacts | 36 months |
| Release SBOM and scan reports | 36 months |
| AutoSkill validation reports | 36 months |

## Immutability

Evidence records are write-once. Modification requires a new record with reference to the original.

## Paper Reproducibility

Evidence supporting paper claims must be retained for at least 36 months after publication to support reproducibility.
